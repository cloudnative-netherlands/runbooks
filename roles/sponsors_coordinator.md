# Sponsor Coordinator

## Role description

The Sponsor Coordinator owns the end-to-end sponsor lifecycle for the event. This includes:

* Developing the sponsorship prospectus.
* Identifying and securing new sponsors
* Re-engaging past partners.
* Building trusted, long-term relationships.
* Managing the pipeline and status of all prospects.
* Coordinating contracts and payments with Finance.
* Ensuring receipt of marketing assets with Communications.
* Aligning booth placement, logistics, and merchandise shipping with the venue team.
* Serving as the primary point of contact through on-site execution and follow-up.
